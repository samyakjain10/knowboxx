ABOUT US

Who are we?

A team of young and determined industry experts graduated from IITs/NITs keen on helping young minds learn. Our team comprises of versatile and dedicated teachers experienced in teaching in American and Indian schools. We stand together to bring quality education to students right in the comfort of their homes!

Our Mission

To help students learn by understanding and visualizing, than through rote learning.

Our vision

To help every individual optimize from their thinking capacity and apply it in real life.

What we do?

Knowboxx is an Indian Ed-Tech company to help K-12 students learn and understand. We aim to provide school children with a LIVE tutoring platform combined with ONLINE teaching. Our programmes are highly interactive, engaging and personalized to empower bright minds. We wish to beat the system of rote learning and help students enjoy studying.
